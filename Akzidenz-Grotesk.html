A book I designed, using text from various sources including Smashing Magazine. Includes works popular during the height of Swiss typographic style, a Designculture interview with Rosemarie Tissi, comparisons between various members of the Akzidenz-Grotesk family, and a brief history of the typeface with a nod to its successor, Helvetica.
