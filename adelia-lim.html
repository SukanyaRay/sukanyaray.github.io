Adelia Lim: a Contemporary Graphic Design Report

This project is a four panel report on a designer whose work I admire and look to for inspiration. Her name is Adelia Lim, and she is a graphic designer based in Singapore. I originally came across Adelia's work in an It's Nice That article, and was fortunate to conduct a Skype interview with her on a Thursday at 10 (P.M. my time, A.M. hers). This report is that interview.


Find more of Adelia's work at adelia-lim.info
